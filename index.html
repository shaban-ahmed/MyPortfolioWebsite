About Me

Hi, I'm Shaaban. I'm an Instructional Designer, eLearning Developer, Learning & Development Professional, or like I want to call myself Learning Architect. I have a bachelor of Automation Engineering from Al-Azhar University. But I'm passionate about helping people learn so I moved to the learning and development field.

I have started working in Learning and Development since 2017 while I was a student; I volunteered for student activities and worked as a freelance instructional designer.

After graduation, I joined the corporate world and started the journey of being an Instructional Designer where I can help people learn and get developed, and where can I practice my mission is creating engaging and effective learning experiences that help people gain the knowledge and skills needed for success.

What can I help you with is to design instructor-led, video-based eLearning training programs, and job aids that develop your people and drive business results.
